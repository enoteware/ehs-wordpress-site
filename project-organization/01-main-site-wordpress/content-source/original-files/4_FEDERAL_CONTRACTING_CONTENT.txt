EM 385-1-1 Safety Requirements:

OSHA Compliance:
We support contractors nationwide with OSHA 1926 construction standards compliance, including development of site-specific safety plans, worker training, hazard assessments, and OSHA inspection support.

═══ MAIN CONTENT SECTION 2: Services ═══
H2 Heading:
SDVOSB EHS Services for Federal Construction
Intro:
We provide comprehensive EHS support for your federal construction projects:
Site Safety and Health Officer (SSHO) Services
Dedicated safety professionals who serve as your on-site safety representative, develop Accident Prevention Plans (APP) and Activity Hazard Analyses (AHA), conduct daily inspections, provide worker training, and ensure EM 385-1-1 compliance. Required on most federal construction projects over $5 million.
Industrial Hygiene Services
Exposure monitoring, air quality assessments, hazardous materials surveys (lead, asbestos, silica), indoor air quality investigations, and compliance support from Certified Industrial Hygienists (CIH).
Construction Safety Program Development
Site-specific safety plans, confined space programs, fall protection plans, excavation safety procedures, crane safety programs, and comprehensive construction safety and health programs.
Environmental Consulting
Environmental compliance support, stormwater pollution prevention plans (SWPPP), spill prevention, hazardous waste management, and environmental inspections.
Worker Safety Training
OSHA 10/30-hour construction training, competent person training, toolbox talks, new hire orientations, and project-specific safety training.
