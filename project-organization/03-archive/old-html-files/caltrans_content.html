Comprehensive Caltrans Safety Services

1. Lead Compliance Plan (LCP) Services

For projects involving lead-containing paint on bridges and structures, we develop comprehensive Lead Compliance Plans per Cal/OSHA Section 1532.1 and Caltrans Special Provisions Section 7-1.02K9. Our CIH-certified staff handle exposure assessment, engineering controls, worker training, and ongoing compliance monitoring.

2. Safety Representative Services

Our Safety Representatives provide on-site safety oversight and Cal/OSHA compliance monitoring for Caltrans construction projects. Services include daily jobsite inspections, safety meeting facilitation, incident investigation, and coordination with Caltrans Resident Engineers per Section 5-1.28 Project Safety Reviews.

  • Daily safety inspections and hazard identification
  • Cal/OSHA compliance verification
  • Safety meeting coordination with Resident Engineer
  • Incident investigation and reporting
  • Job Hazard Analysis (JHA) review per Section 5-1.29
  • Worker safety training and orientation

3. Public Health and Safety Plan (PHSP)

We develop comprehensive Public Health and Safety Plans for Caltrans projects requiring stakeholder coordination and public safety protocols. Our plans address construction impacts on surrounding communities, emergency response procedures, and regulatory compliance for projects near schools, hospitals, and residential areas.

  • Public safety impact assessment
  • Traffic control and pedestrian safety measures
  • Emergency response and evacuation procedures
  • Stakeholder communication protocols
  • Environmental health considerations
  • Regulatory agency coordination

4. Work Area Monitoring (WAM) - Bridge Projects

For bridge rehabilitation and painting projects, we provide Work Area Monitoring services including air quality monitoring, worker exposure assessment, and real-time compliance verification. Our monitoring programs ensure contractor adherence to Lead Compliance Plans and protect workers from lead, silica, and other construction hazards.

  • Personal and area air monitoring for lead and silica
  • Real-time results and exposure tracking
  • Engineering control verification
  • Respiratory protection program oversight
  • Cal/OSHA compliance documentation
  • Caltrans reporting per project specifications

5. Health & Safety Plan (HSP) Development

We create site-specific Health & Safety Plans tailored to your Caltrans project requirements. Our plans integrate Cal/OSHA regulations, Caltrans Standard Specifications, and project-specific hazard controls to provide comprehensive safety management throughout construction.

  • Project-specific hazard identification
  • Emergency response and evacuation procedures
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) requirements
  • Confined space entry protocols
  • Excavation and trenching safety
  • Fall protection and scaffolding requirements
  • Hazard communication and training programs

6. Safety Quality Control Manager (SQCM)

Our Safety Quality Control Managers provide senior-level safety oversight and ensure quality assurance for all safety-related activities on Caltrans projects. SQCM services include safety program audits, documentation review, Cal/OSHA compliance verification, and coordination with project quality control teams.

  • Safety program quality assurance
  • Cal/OSHA compliance audits
  • Documentation review and verification
  • Corrective action tracking
  • Safety training program oversight
  • Coordination with project QC/QA teams
  • Caltrans reporting and submittals
