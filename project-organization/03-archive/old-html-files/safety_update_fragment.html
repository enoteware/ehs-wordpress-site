Construction Safety Services Across All Market Segments

Our SDVOSB certification helps prime contractors meet small business subcontracting goals while ensuring full safety compliance on military installations throughout California and nationwide.

  • SSHO staffing
  • Accident Prevention Plan (APP) development
  • Activity Hazard Analysis (AHA) creation

  • EM 385-1-1 compliance

California Public Works & Caltrans

Expert Knowledge of Federal & California Safety Requirements

Our construction safety consultants are experts in both federal and California-specific safety regulations:

EM 385-1-1 Safety Requirements

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers Safety and Health Requirements Manual (EM 385-1-1) governs all federal military construction. Our team has extensive experience developing Accident Prevention Plans (APP), Activity Hazard Analyses (AHA), conducting safety inspections, and ensuring full compliance with EM 385-1-1 on USACE and NAVFAC projects.

Cal/OSHA Expertise

California has some of the most stringent construction safety requirements in the nation. Our Certified Industrial Hygienists and Certified Safety Professionals are experts in Cal/OSHA Title 8 construction standards, including lead compliance, silica exposure, confined space entry, fall protection, excavation safety, and more.

OSHA Compliance

We support contractors nationwide with OSHA 1926 construction standards compliance, including development of site-specific safety plans, worker training, hazard assessments, and OSHA inspection support.
