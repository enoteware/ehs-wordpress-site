    • Overview

    EHS Analytical provides comprehensive Site Safety and Health Officer (SSHO) services for federal construction projects. Our certified safety professionals ensure full compliance with EM 385-1-1 and other federal safety requirements.

    With extensive experience in federal contracting and SDVOSB certification, we deliver safety oversight that protects workers, ensures compliance, and keeps projects on schedule.

    Our Services

    Daily Safety Inspections

    Comprehensive daily inspections of construction sites to identify and mitigate safety hazards before they cause incidents.

    Safety Program Development

    Development and implementation of site-specific safety programs that meet EM 385-1-1 requirements and project specifications.

    Training and Education

    On-site safety training for construction crews, covering topics from fall protection to hazardous materials handling.

    Compliance

    Our SSHO services ensure full compliance with:

    • EM 385-1-1 (USACE Safety and Health Requirements Manual)
    • OSHA Construction Standards (29 CFR 1926)
    • Project-specific safety requirements
    • Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR)

    Certifications

    Our SSHO team holds the following certifications:

    • CSP - Certified Safety Professional
    • CHST - Construction Health & Safety Technician
    • SDVOSB - Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business
    • DVBE - Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise (#2017031)
